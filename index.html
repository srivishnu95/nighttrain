Night Train

your one stop badminton centre.

Night Train is your one-stop badminton center, providing everything from facilities, training academy, tournament event management, and sports travel.

We aspire to provide all resources to help any shuttlers regardless of their level of expertise.

Creating champions at our academy

Our generalist training is suitable for all badminton enthusiasts to learn, brushing up on skills and techniques under the guidance of our NIS-certified coaches. We also offer private, custom-tailored training suited for students with irregular schedules or people requiring a more customized coaching regime.

Training

Night Train helps you provide a helping hand in executing winning strategies in the court. Be it on the work:

 
  • on your footwork,
  • conditional training,
  • calming routines during rallies, or
  • practicing different shots,

get the best guidance from our world-class team of coaches.

Channel the energy

Competitions are crucial in shaping overall development. Our coaches encourage students to participate in various competitions. By introducing competitive leagues and tournaments that are open to players of all ages and levels, we create opportunities to compete and raise everyone’s game. This helps students distinguish between effort and ability. Playing against others widens their experience and ability to handle different types of players and different strategies.

Leveling up the juniors

We start children as young as the age of 6 onwards.
The best way to take the sport to the next level is to ensure that our juniors level up faster. To a point where they don’t just master the sport - they innovate the sport. Through our national player network, we are developing new ways of giving juniors access to better learning tools.

Coaching lessons are irrespective of public holidays. Students will receive a notification via their registered email or mobile on holiday dates.


A better play is guaranteed if you continue to take the instructional tips with you and continue on the right path of practicing the prescribed techniques. Consistency, patience, willingness to learn, and effort are the keys to improving your game.


Surfacing the undercurrent

With each level upgrade, the training module intensifies with a higher level coach. It is recommended that you commit to the minimum number of sessions to show significant progress.

Court Booking

Flooring

All our badminton courts are synthetic layered with wooden flooring. This distributes stress evenly, making performance efficient for players.


It safeguards the interest of badminton players by giving good grip. It provides a buffer to the players by reducing the impact if players fall, thus reducing injuries.
